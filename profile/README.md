**Behavioral Health, Business Consulting & Coaching Services**

---

![Our GitHub Game](../game.gif)

We are a multidisciplinary practice based in **Northwest Arkansas** providing evidence-based behavioral health services, business consulting, and professional coaching.

### What We Do

- **Behavioral Health Services** — Individual therapy, couples counseling, family therapy, and clinical assessments using CBT, DBT, ACT, and other evidence-based modalities
- **Business Consulting** — Strategic planning, organizational development, and operational excellence for healthcare and professional services
- **Coaching Services** — Executive coaching, career coaching, wellness coaching, and personal development

### Our Commitment

- HIPAA-compliant infrastructure and clinical workflows
- Evidence-based treatment approaches backed by validated screening tools
- Accessible telehealth services across Arkansas
